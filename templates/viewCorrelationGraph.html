What is Correlation?

In general, correlation is a way to measure the strength of the relationship between variables. A correlation between variables indicates that as one variable changes in value, the other variable tends to change in a specific direction.

In terms of Stock Analytics, a correlation graph would show us the positive or negative relationship amongst different stocks. This means that when a certain stock price goes up, then another stock price might go up too which denotes a positive relationship between them; or another stock price may go down which denotes a negative relationship between them.

The following chart refreshes daily as it keeps learning to make better predictions each day. The graph below would let you get a gist of the relationships between different stocks. A correlation value of less than 0 indicates that the stocks are correlated in a negative direction and a value greater than 0 indicates that they are correlated in a positive direction.
